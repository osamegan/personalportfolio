What I've Created

I N

S C H O O L

Throughout this year I have used art in multiple
projects. The main one was when we had to make a diorama based off the interactive story we were making.
My first set of partners I painted a shoebox to give off a creepy graveyard feel then added some fake
little trees, a metal house, and some small skeleton bones. We had to get creative because we wanted
things to be proportional but also to where you could understand what the scene was. Then with my second
partner she baked most of the items we put in the diorama.

O N

M Y

O W N

In my spare time I like to
draw quite often. It is one way I am able to express my emotions and produce
artwork I really love. I rarely draw animals and full body sketches due to the
fact that I'm not good at it. I mainly draw faces and other random doodles i find.
Most of what I draw is either very dark or very emotional. They usually have a deep
meaning to them. Sometimes the meaning is obvious and other times not so much.
I also love baking and cooking. It is just so much fun and you get yummy treats
afterwards. It's always a nice reward after lots of hard work.
