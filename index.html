Chess.io

My first project, we were asked to create a video game and present it to the school. My partner and I created a game like chess but with many differences. One of the changes we made was it could have up to four players, one in each corner. We also reduced the amount of pieces you have. Each piece looks like a different pacman character. For instance, one may look like King PacMan while another looks like a scared ghost. In our game you would have 7 pieces rather than 16 pieces for each player. Each player also had different colors, they could choose between orange, pink, blue, and red.

Interactive Story

The next project, I made an interactive story about a girl who has to deal with lots and you go along with her for the ride. You start out choosing if you will play as Annie (the main character), her sister, or her mom. There were two main branches to the story that sent you down completely different paths, you either go with a man you meet and look for your sister or join a cult and harness and learn to control your powers.

Calculator

Another project I worked on was a calculator, a dropdown box would ask you to put in a number first. Then it would ask for a symbol then another number. It then would solve the equation and dropdown the correct answer.

Advent of Code

Lastly, in this project I was given an extremely long list of hundreds of numbers. My job was to create a program that would find the total of the numbers. All but the first number had a plus or minus in front of it which meant it would either be added or subtracted from the previous total.
